Originally from France, Lavie grew up in a small town in the South of Portugal where Techno, Minimal and Trance were a big part of his life. Roaming through the nights of Portugal, from south to north in underground parties and outdoor raves. And although he would vibrate and lose himself with every Dj set, he always felt like something was missing. And it wasn't until he arrived in Berlin and experienced such a vivid and diverse House culture that he found his true calling.
Nowadays, House is what makes him vibrate. From Nu-Disco to Deep House, Jazz infused House, Chicago, Detroit, Soul, Funk, Rock, Hip Hop, Nu-Wave, Nu-Jazz or Old School House music. Anything that has some dope/swingy drums and a soulful bass line will make his heart beat."

- Understanding the Roots of ClubCulture @ Talking Tacheles
